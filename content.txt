*Motion Design System*

In the "Hidden Layers" project, our team developed a cohesive motion design system 
for the 2024 Design & AI Conference. 
We reinterpreted the institution’s corporate identity to create a flexible design framework, 
setting clear guidelines for visual elements, motion, and composition. My primary role was in the conceptual phase, 
where I helped shape the overall look and feel by defining the visual rules.

Production
During production, I focused on creating generative background visuals based on our motion design system, 
establishing the foundation for the conference’s visual identity. In the role of creative director, 
I contributed by overseeing the organization and coordination of the production process to ensure consistency and timely delivery.

*Umkrempler 2k-6*
Umkrempler 2k-6 is an audio-visual performance by Lio Vast and Paul Kimmerl.

​The performance explores and contrasts emotional states beyond everyday perception, interpreting philosophical concepts within the tension between chaos and control.
Our focus lies in evoking emotional experiences that transcend culturally dominant base emotions shaped by pop culture and social media. This immersive approach does not rely on academic context or conceptual explanation but instead invites interpretation through direct experience—encouraging reflection regardless of background, resources, or identity.
